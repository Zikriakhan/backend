Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What services does CUCSR provide?

CUCSR offers comprehensive recruitment and HR solutions including local & overseas manpower recruitment, HR consultancy, screening & testing, training & capacity building, graduate placement programs, documentation & visa processing, and career counseling.

Which countries do you recruit for?

We recruit for organizations across the GCC region (UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, Oman, Bahrain), Europe (UK, Germany, France, Spain, Italy, Netherlands), Asia Pacific (Singapore, Malaysia, Hong Kong, Japan, South Korea, Australia), and North America (USA, Canada).

What industries do you specialize in?

We specialize in Construction & Infrastructure, Oil & Gas, Healthcare & Nursing, IT & Software Engineering, Marketing & Communication, Retail & Consumer Goods, Sales & Business Development, Supply Chain & Logistics, E-commerce, Accounts & Finance, and various Business Support roles.

How long does the recruitment process take?

The recruitment timeline varies based on the position, industry, and destination country. Typically, from initial screening to deployment, the process takes 4-8 weeks for most positions, subject to visa processing times and client requirements.

What makes CUCSR different from other recruitment agencies?

As part of City University of Science & Information Technology (CUSIT), we have direct access to thousands of graduates, strong industry-academia linkages, research facilities, and training resources. Our ethical recruitment standards, transparent processes, and post-placement support set us apart.

Do you provide post-placement support?

Yes, we provide comprehensive post-placement support including follow-up with both employers and candidates, assistance with any issues that may arise, and continuous engagement to ensure successful long-term placements.

How can employers partner with CUCSR?

Employers can contact us through our website, email, or phone to discuss their hiring needs. We'll schedule a consultation to understand your requirements and propose a tailored recruitment solution.

How can job seekers register with CUCSR?

Job seekers can register through our website, visit our office, or contact us via email/phone. We'll guide you through the registration process, skills assessment, and career counseling to match you with suitable opportunities.
